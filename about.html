About Us




Who We Are

Sakura Japanese Restaurant is the best Japanese and Chinese restaurant in Jackson Tennessee. We are a family owned restaurant that has had years of experience (established in 1998) in working and making your favorite foods. Here at Sakura, our employees will always provide excellent service, and our chef's and cook's will always make your food perfectly. When ever you need some good food, we are always here!
